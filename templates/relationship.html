Understanding Relationships

Explore the dynamics, challenges, and support options for healthy relationships.

What is a Relationship?

A relationship is a connection or bond between individuals that offers emotional support, comfort, and companionship. Life often feels easier when you have someone to share your highs and lows with...

Types of Relationships

  • Family relationships
  • Friendships
  • Acquaintances
  • Romantic relationships
  • Sexual relationships
  • Work relationships
  • Situational relationships

Signs and Symptoms

  • Communication Problems
  • Emotional Disconnection
  • Frequent Arguments
  • Infidelity
  • Physical Abuse
  • Trust Issues

Detailed Symptoms

  • Communication difficulties: Misunderstandings may lead to frustration and emotional distance.
  • Trust issues: Jealousy from past betrayals.
  • Intimacy problems: Lack of emotional/physical closeness.
  • Frequent conflicts: Escalation into arguments or violence.
  • Emotional withdrawal: Detachment and lack of connection.
  • Goal/value conflicts: Tension from incompatibility.
  • Emotional distress: Anxiety, depression, or low self-esteem.

Causes and Challenges

Common Causes:

  • Communication gaps
  • Trust breakdown
  • Value or goal differences
  • Lack of intimacy
  • Past traumas
  • Major life changes
  • Mental health concerns
