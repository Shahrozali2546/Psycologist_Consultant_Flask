Chemical Addiction (Substance Use)

  • Alcohol
  • Opioids (Heroin, Oxycodone, Morphine)
  • Cannabis
  • Cocaine
  • Tobacco
  • Other narcotics

Behavioral Addiction

  • Shopping
  • Exercise
  • Sex
  • Television
  • Social media

Signs and Symptoms of Addiction

  • Aggressive behavior
  • Loss of appetite
  • Decline in work/school performance
  • Disturbed sleep, strained relationships
  • Change in social circles, dishonesty, risky behavior
  • Neglecting responsibilities
  • Physical appearance changes
  • Withdrawal symptoms: nausea, tremors, etc.

Addiction Treatment Approaches

  • Detoxification: Supervised withdrawal phase
  • Counseling: CBT, DBT, REBT, Motivational Interviewing
  • Medication-Assisted Treatment: Methadone, Buprenorphine, etc.
  • Support Groups: AA, NA, group therapy
  • Rehab: Inpatient & outpatient programs
Benefits of Online Counseling
  • Safe, private environment
  • Emotional healing & relapse prevention
  • Improved relationships & family support
  • Accessible, affordable therapy sessions
